FAQs

  • Do you have any plan to publish modifications to the problem during the contest?

    After the lightning round, a new set of problems with a complication will be introduced that will require more work to solve, aside from that: no plans.

  • Is there a maximum length for route plans?

    Yes, you may enter 18n doors where n is the number of rooms.
